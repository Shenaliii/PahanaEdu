Pahana Education Help Center

Need help? Refer to your role below and find relevant guidance.

Cashier Guide

  • Login: Use your cashier credentials on the login screen.
  • Sell Books: Search by title or ISBN, add to cart, and finalize sale.
  • Print Receipt: Automatically prompts after sale completion.
  • Sales History: Access your past transactions anytime.
  • Logout: Click profile > Logout.

Admin Guide

  • User Management: Add, update, or remove cashiers.
  • Inventory Control: Manage books, pricing, and stock levels.
  • Reports: Generate sales reports by day, week, or month.
  • Settings: Update store details, password, and preferences.
  • Admin Logs: Track system activities.

Troubleshooting

  • Forgot Password: Contact your administrator to reset it.
  • System Lag: Refresh your browser or check internet connectivity.
  • Printing Errors: Reconnect printer or verify drivers.
